REVIEW the movie Empire Records (1995)

I think Ford vs Ferrari is a great movie. It teaches us the history between Ford and Ferrari and how Ford was able to beat Ferrari in the 24 Hour Le Mans race. I watched the movie when it came out and I still watch it. My favorite part in the movie is when they are testing the car around the track trying to make it better. If you are into cars or I would recemond watching this movie.
